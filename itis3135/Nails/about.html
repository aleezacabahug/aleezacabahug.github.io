Sophia Park is a talented nail technician based in Charlotte, NC, specializing in GEL-X mobile nail services and custom press-ons. As a proud University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC) student, Sophia combines her passion for artistry and beauty to provide clients with convenient, high-quality nail services wherever they are. Her mobile service ensures flexibility for busy individuals, and her press-on nails offer a customizable, stylish option for any occasion. With a commitment to detail and customer satisfaction, Sophia has built a reputation for creating stunning, long-lasting nail designs tailored to each client's unique preferences.
