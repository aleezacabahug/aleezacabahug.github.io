How to Play Valorant

Valorant involves two teams of five players: one team defends bomb sites, and the other attempts to plant a spike at a site. Each player selects an agent with unique abilities.

  • Pick an agent that complements your team.
  • Use abilities to control spaces and support teammates.
  • Communicate effectively for strategic plays.
