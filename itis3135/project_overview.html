Project Name:

Nails by Sophia (GEL-X Mobile and Press-On Nails)

Project Overview

Describe your proposed website:

This website is for Nails by Sophia, a mobile nail tech business specializing in GEL-X nail services and custom press-ons, located in Charlotte, NC. The website will allow clients to browse through nail design galleries, book appointments, purchase press-on nails, and get in touch with Sophia for inquiries or custom requests.

Purpose:

The purpose of the website is to provide an online platform where customers can explore her nail services, book appointments, buy custom press-ons, and view her work. It also offers information about mobile services and press-ons for those unfamiliar with the options.

Intended Users of the Website:

The website is designed for anyone in the Charlotte, NC area looking for mobile nail services, as well as individuals nationwide interested in purchasing custom press-on nails. Target users include busy professionals, students, and beauty enthusiasts who value convenience and personalized service.

Overview of the Content of the Website:

  • Home: Introduction to Sophia's business and a brief description of her services.
  • Services: Detailed explanation of GEL-X and press-on nail services.
  • Gallery: A visual portfolio showcasing Sophia's nail designs.
  • Book Now: Appointment booking system for mobile nail services.
  • Shop: Online store for custom press-on nails.
  • Contact: Her contact details, including social media links, email, and phone number.
  • FAQ: Frequently asked questions about the services, pricing, and policies.

Page Design

  • Home Page:

    Purpose: Introduce the business, provide an overview of services, and direct users to other parts of the site.

    Content: Brief welcome message, description of services, navigation links to Services, Gallery, Shop, and Contact.

  • Services Page:

    Purpose: Detail the different services offered, including mobile GEL-X services and custom press-on nails.

    Content: Descriptions of each service, prices, and links to the Book Now page for GEL-X services or Shop for press-ons.

  • Gallery Page:

    Purpose: Display a portfolio of nail work.

    Content: High-quality images of previous designs, organized in categories for GEL-X and press-ons.

  • Book Now Page:

    Purpose: Allow customers to book mobile nail appointments with Sophia.

    Content: An online booking form with available dates, service options, and confirmation details.

  • Shop Page:

    Purpose: Provide an e-commerce platform for customers to buy press-on nails.

    Content: Display press-on nail sets, filtering options by size and style, product details, and a checkout process.

  • Contact Page:

    Purpose: Offer ways for customers to get in touch with Sophia for appointments or inquiries.

    Content: Email address, phone number, social media links (Instagram, LinkedIn), and a contact form.

  • FAQ Page:

    Purpose: Answer common questions about services, booking, payments, and policies.

    Content: A list of frequently asked questions with detailed answers.

Dynamic Functionality on the Website

The dynamic functionality for the “Nails by Sophia” website will focus on enhancing user interaction and streamlining the booking and purchasing processes.
