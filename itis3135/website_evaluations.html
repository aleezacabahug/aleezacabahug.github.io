Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's official website showcases its products with a sleek and modern design.

  • Contrast: The website uses high contrast between text and background, making it easy to read.
  • Repetition: The same fonts and buttons are used consistently across the site.
  • Alignment: Everything is perfectly aligned, giving the site a polished and professional look.
  • Proximity: Related products and information are grouped together, making navigation intuitive.

Ling's Cars

Ling's Cars website is an example of an over-the-top, chaotic design that overwhelms users.

  • Contrast: The site lacks good contrast, with too many colors competing for attention.
  • Repetition: There's little consistency in fonts or styles, creating a jarring experience.
  • Alignment: Poor alignment makes it difficult to follow any logical layout.
  • Proximity: Unrelated elements are often clumped together, confusing users.
